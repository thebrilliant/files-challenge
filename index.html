L

L is a mysterious young man from the anime Death Note. Despite having only a handful of people know what he looks like, he's the world's top detective. He's a genius that has solved every case he had ever worked on. He's a bit of a recluse with lots of quirks. L is constantly sleep deprived with black circles under his eyes and lives on a constant sugar high. L is a rival to the character Light Yagami, the suspect in L's last case. Although both characters try to catch the other, they end up working together until Light kills L.

L is a main character in Death Note. Death Note was originally a graphic novel serialized in the Japanese magazine, Shounen Jump in 2003. The manga series was made into an anime and released in 2006.
